I joined the business in 2012 as an Auction Specialist, my interview was legendary. I arrived from a rival auction house, Jamie Cooke (MD) was convinced I was a spy for at least the first year! I helped to develop the West Midlands region; ranging from the recruitment of agents to training said agents, and of course Sales. In 2014 I was promoted to Senior Auction Specialist and then again to Midlands Area Team Leader a year later. I oversaw 20 live auction events per year with a team of 5, whilst maintaining my position as Top Seller for 5 consecutive years. In 2019 I was promoted to Regional Performance Manager initially with a team of 8 covering Yorkshire and West Midlands regions. Today I have 14 direct reports covering The Midlands, West Midlands and The South. I love the comradery and enjoy the fast pace nature of the business.
