I joined the business in 2012 as an Auction Specialist. I arrived from a rival auction houseand Jamie was convinced I was a spy for at least the first year!
I helped to develop the West Midlands region, ranging from the recruitment of agents to training and sales. In 2014 I was promoted to Senior Auction Specialist and then again to Midlands Area Team Leader a year later. I oversaw 20 live auction events per year with a team of 5, whilst maintaining my position as Top Seller for 5 consecutive years.
In 2019 I waspromoted to Regional Performance Manager with a team of 8 covering the Yorkshire and West Midlands regions. Today I have 14 direct reports covering The Midlands, West Midlands and The South.
I love the comradery and enjoy the fast-paced nature of the business. I know it sounds cheesy, but you really feel as though you’re part of something special here.

I started with iamproperty in 2010 as an Auction Specialist for iamsold. I then moved intoBusiness Development and for a number of years recruited Partner Agents into the network. When we landed our first corporate client I spent some time integrating the aligned objectives before recruiting internally for someone to manage the day to day running of that division.
I was then tasked with supporting the growth of our Southern region and headed up the internal and external strategy, until a restructure of the sales operation gave me an opportunity to lead our Account Management teams. Moving forward, my goals are to ensure that we create as much value possible of our Partner Agents through aligned objectives and account plans, and ensure the delivery of the product range to the best possible standards.
Despite our huge growth and headcount, the company has maintained its family culture and great working environment.
